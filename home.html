WELCOME TO ROYALE HOTEL

Royale Hotel is among the four star luxury hotels in the world offering state of art meeting amenities and services. Our hotel is located in the Western part of Ghana-an irresistible breathing space destination with miles of unpigmented beaches.

One can find great hospitality and comfort in this hotel. This hotel owns great facilities which include restaurant, bar, gym and a swimming pool for our guest.

All of our 150 guest room facility comprise of fast speed internet service, desktop computer for research, air-conditioning, a refined bathroom with fixed showers, television connected to a dstv cable and a work desk. Our hotel has a large conference center which can cater for up to 80 people with flexible setups to suit your needs.

The privately owned Royale Hotel has an extraordinary charm, style and individuality. But most important of all is that the Royale Hotel will really make you feel at home. We care about you, we want you to feel more than welcome, and we want you to leave with treasured memories of a truly wonderful stay. This is why the friendly, courteous and helpful staff of the Royale Hotel is there for you, day and night.
